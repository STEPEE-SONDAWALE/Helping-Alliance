Frequently Asked Questions

How do I donate items?

Click on "Donate Items", select the category, and fill out the form. We'll guide you for pickup or drop-off.

Do I need an account to donate?

No. You can donate without an account, but creating one lets you track donations.

What items can I donate?

Clothes, books, toys, and other safe and usable household goods.
